Hi! I'm Joseph, the founder of MapleDiv, a software development company based in Portland, OR.

A versatile professional bridging the realms of Software Engineering and UX Design, with a robust background in computer science and a creative mindset honed through years of designing user-centric experiences, I bring a unique skill set to the table.

In my 13+ years in the tech industry, I've seamlessly blended the analytical mindset of a Software Engineer with the empathetic approach of a UX Designer. My journey involves architecting and implementing scalable software solutions while ensuring they resonate with end-users on an intuitive level.

As a Software Engineer, I've delved into diverse projects, coding with proficiency in languages such as Python, JavaScript, and SQL. From backend development, where efficiency and reliability are paramount, to frontend interfaces where user engagement takes center stage, I've navigated the full software development life cycle with tools like Flask, React.JS, and Node.JS.

On the UX Design front, my passion lies in understanding user behavior, conducting meticulous research, and translating insights into seamless and delightful interfaces. Wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing are integral parts of my process, ensuring that the end product not only functions flawlessly but also provides an exceptional user experience.

I thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments where the intersection of technology and design sparks innovation. My commitment to staying abreast of industry trends allows me to bring cutting-edge solutions to every project. Whether it's writing elegant code or crafting visually appealing and intuitive interfaces, I'm dedicated to creating digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.
